Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

How does purchasing minutes work?

Minutes are purchased in advance through the App Store. Pricing is determined by the current Google Vertex rates for Gemini Live.

Is my data used to train the AI?

No. Since this is a paid service, your interactions (audio and camera feed) are private and are not used to improve or train Google's AI models.

What is required to use the app?

You only need a stable internet connection and to grant the app permission to access your camera and microphone.
